Hip Ultrasound Calculator

Desktop Application for Booking Hip Ultrasounds in Newborns

All newborns at increased risk for developmental dysplasia of the hips (DDH) should be screened for DDH by hip ultrasound at 46 weeks of gestation. The DDH Hip Ultrasound Calculator will help determine the appropriate booking date (at least 46 weeks of gestation) based on the newborn's date of birth and gestational age at birth.
